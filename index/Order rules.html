Order rules

 

 

 

important hint


The authorization of this platform to cooperate with third-party shopping platform merchants is currently limited to the United Arab Emirates. Users from other regions are not allowed to perform malicious operations to earn order commissions

The product order intelligent system will identify the user's IP and perform relevant detection. If it is detected that the account is a non-UAE user, the risk control system will control the illegal account


 

 

 

 

Order rules

 

 

Commodity order update time: 00:00 every morning  (High commission orders besides)

 

1. Usually the amount of each bulk order is 30% to 90% of the account balance. For example: the account balance is 1000 TRX, and the product amount is between 300 and 900 TRX

 

2. The order is matched to the corresponding merchant within 1 minute. Users occasionally match rush orders from merchants with orders below 30% of their account balance or above 90% of their account balance. Commissions for rush orders and overorders are much higher than for individual regular orders. Only when the account balance exceeds 10 TRX, it is possible to match urgent orders and high commission orders. (The percentage of commission is based on the actual quotation of the merchant)

 

3. The minimum commission for merchant orders is US$0.01. If the amount is less than US$0.01, it will be calculated at US$0.01

 

 

VIP level automatic upgrade

1.account upgrade needs to accumulate more than 1,000 TRX commission and use the account invitation code to invite 3 users. The system will automatically upgrade the account

2.If the user completes all product orders in the account on the day, the credit point will be increased by 1. When the account credit score reaches 10 points, the system will automatically upgrade the account VIP level

If all product orders in the account are not completed on the day, 1 point of credit will be deducted (including all associated invitation code accounts)

 

 

The account is divided into 4 levels, and the daily order quantity and commission rate of different account levels are different

 

 

VIP1

66 orders per day. 0.12% commission per order

 

VIP2

68 orders per day. 0.13% commission per order

 

VIP3

70 orders per day. 0.14% commission per order

 

VIP4

72 orders per day. 0.15% commission per order

 

The commission for excess orders and rush orders is based on the merchant's actual quotation

 

 

Account Credit: The order must be submitted immediately after matching the product. If the user maliciously fails to click to submit the order many times, the system will reduce the account credit if the order payment time limit is exceeded and the order system is not completed. Account top-ups will be restricted

 

If the account is matched to an excess order.The system will increase the credit of the account if the user completes the order within the countdown of commodity payment.High credit accounts will be preferentially matched to merchants with high commission orders

 

Account recharge limits: There are differences in the recharge limits for different levels of VIP accounts. The system will continuously update the recharge limits of different accounts based on the total amount of merchants and the total amount of all user accounts on the day. (Except for excess orders and high commission orders)

 

Withdrawal: Need to complete all orders matched on the day

Withdrawal handling fee: There is no handling fee for withdrawals with an amount less than 1,000 TRX. For withdrawals of more than 1,000 TRX, a 2% fee will be charged based on the withdrawal amount

 

 

After the user completes each commodity order, the payment system of the platform will automatically transfer the commission to the user's account according to the actualcommission of the commodity. After the user completes all orders for the day. Please fill in thecorresponding information in the withdrawal column for withdrawal, and the payment system will automatically deduct the commission prepaid by the merchant. The user's withdrawal account will receive the corresponding withdrawal funds within the specified time
